About

Born and raised in a small city called Hyderabad, India, Started with Bollywood dancing at the age of 18, Started breaking at the age of 19, Started Teaching from age of 20.

Created some projects like : K.O.M (2013)- knowledge of movement, a grass root level training for school students in all local schools. B.B.O.Y workshop (2011) - An annual Breaking workshop/ Battle series. Created the first Hip-hop national Battle series and workshops in the state called Celestial Cyphers Vol #1, #2 (2012, 2013).

Won many local and national championships, but most prominent ones are Blame it on the boogie (2013) - all styles champion, Youdh (2013) - House and all styles runner up, best choreographer award in NASA (National Association of Students of Architecture) 2014.

Created two crews in south India - Royal style Unit (2012) - Breaking crew, Dragon Clan (2012) - All styles crew.

Formed K&D( Komet & Dragon - 2014) an organization dedicated to empower women through Hip-hop, created battle series called 'Soul Invasion #1, #2' (2013, 2014)
